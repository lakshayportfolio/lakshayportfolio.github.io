Trident Ltd

Lakshay Anand Jan 31, 2022 · 10 min read

Trident Limited, the flagship company of the Trident Group, is a leading manufacturer of yarn, Bath Linen, Bed Linen, wheat straw-based paper, Chemicals, and Captive Power. Currently, the company has manufacturing facilities in Barnala (Punjab) and Budhni (Madhya Pradesh).

Trident’s clientele comprises well-known institutional brands as well as retail brands. The home textiles segment serves Target Global Sourcing Ltd, Wal-Mart Stores Inc., IKEA Supply AG, and Amazon Inc, among others. Trident earned an average of around 54% of its home textile revenue from the top five customers over the last three years ended FY21.

They have Constantly Improved their Top Line, another point to consider here is that they derive more than 50% of sales in the form of exports, as can be seen from the graph below.



They Majorly Operate in Three Revenue Segments
• Home Textiles: It includes Bath and Bed linen. Some of the products are Comforters, Solid/ Printed Sheets, Decorative pillows, etc
• Yarn: 100% cotton combed yarn, Slub yarn, Open-End yarn, Blended yarn, etc.
• Paper: Includes Branded copiers, Maplitho paper, Bible and offset printing paper.

They are the market leader in the US Terry Towel category which is a Crown Jewel product in their Product portfolio.



Growth Drivers for the Indian Textile Industry
• Favorable Government Policies: Focus on Make in India, Export Enhancement, Scheme of Setup of Mega Textile parks.
• Textiles a dominant Industry: Employs 45 million people across the Value Chain, Strong Government Support for Industry.
• Competitive cost of Production: Competitive labor, Power and raw material cost, and Access to advanced technology under textile upgradation fund.
• Global friendly environment: Stable democracy and Evolving Practices in line with MNCs.

My Narrative and Valuation

1) Export Driven Revenues: As Trident derives on an average more than 50% of their revenues in the form of exports and that too majorly from US Market in which they command Leadership in the terry towels Category, they will be able to maintain the current trend of growth in revenues.

2) EBIT Margins: Operating Margins have been 11% to 13% during the past five years because of disruptions in Supply Chain and Inflationary pressures on the Raw material but in the long run it's going to be Stable at around 12%.



3) Cost of Capital: Cost of equity is calculated using the CAPM approach under which risk premium is calculated using Market premium based on the return of NSE, and Beta is calculated using Regression Beta. The cost of debt is calculated based on the historical trend of their outstanding debt at 5.5%(post-tax). The overall Cost of Capital based on the capital Structure of the company is 10.06%.
